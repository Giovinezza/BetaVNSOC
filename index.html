What we are :

Socialists

We fight for the good of society as a whole, of the working nation, of the common man. It is the one most essential element in our movement, and hence the name "Vietnam Social Movement".

Proletarian (Class) Nationalists

We focus on the working class nation, and its struggle for self-determination and national identity. We emphasizes the unity of the working class across and within national boundaries, but reject globalism. We call for the destruction of capitalist systems that exploit the working class. We are against bourgeoisie nationalism, which had been serving the interests of the ruling class at the expense of the working class, and was dialectically overthrown my modern neo-liberalism.

True Progressives

We embrace the revolution and are revolutionaries ourselves. Choosing to be revolutionaries means rejecting the rot of the current capitalist societies and systems, while also rejecting the old structures under feudalism or monarchical societies. We refuse to step back, only take inspirations and lessons.
